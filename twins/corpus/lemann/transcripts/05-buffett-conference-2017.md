# Warren Buffett Q&A Transcript — Brazil Conference with Jorge Paulo Lemann (2017)

**Source:** Kingswell | April 2017
**Participants:** Warren Buffett, Jorge Paulo Lemann
**Moderator:** Nitin Nohria
**Context:** Pre-Kraft Heinz merger complications

---

## Partnership Formation

### How Buffett & Lemann Met
- Mutual board membership at Gillette
- Buffett: Considers it "one of the larger mistakes in my life" they didn't partner sooner
- "He and I are on the same wavelength"

### The Heinz Deal (2013)
- Setting: Colorado, heading to the plane
- Lemann proposed Heinz casually
- Buffett: "Sounds good to me!"
- Lemann sent: one page of financial terms + one page of governance terms
- Buffett didn't need to change a word — comparable to working with Charlie Munger

---

## Partnership Philosophy

> "Who you have as partners in life — and you start, in most cases, with your spouses — it's a lot more fun and it actually is a lot more profitable to have wonderful partners. But the most important thing is it's just a lot more fun in life." — Warren Buffett

**Key Principles:**
- Only work with people you genuinely enjoy
- Partnership quality directly impacts profitability and life satisfaction
- Time and love are the two things money cannot buy

---

## Lemann on Success & Time

### The Gillette Board Conversation
Buffett asked if Lemann was happy selling his bank:

> "Because I really didn't want to be somebody running a Goldman Sachs. I'd much rather be like him. You have better control of your time, you have a better sense of humor, and you're much richer."

### Buffett's Definition of Wealth
Buffett showed Lemann his empty calendar:

> "Look at how rich I am! I don't have much to do. I only do what I like and I only do it with my friends. So I'm very, very rich."

**Impact:** Lemann's wife still references this, noting the difference in Lemann's schedule vs. Buffett's.
